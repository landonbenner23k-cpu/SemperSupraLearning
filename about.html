About Semper Supra Learning

Our Mission & Values

Our Story

Semper Supra Learning was founded with a deeply personal mission. Our founder was inspired by their grandmother— an immigrant from Chihuahua, Mexico—who despite facing tremendous obstacles, possessed an unquenchable thirst for learning. Though circumstances limited her own educational opportunities, she instilled in her family the belief that education is a fundamental right and the pathway to a better future.

Her legacy of resilience, determination, and hope became the foundation for Semper Supra Learning. We created this organization to honor her memory by ensuring that no Latino student would face the barriers she did—that every young person would have access to the quality education and support they deserve.

Our Mission

Semper Supra Learning is dedicated to providing free, high-quality tutoring services to Latino middle and high school students who are struggling academically. We believe that every student deserves access to quality education and support, regardless of their socioeconomic background. We honor the dreams of those who came before us by lifting up the next generation.

What We Do

We offer personalized tutoring in core subjects including:

  • 🔢 Mathematics

  • 🌍 Social Studies & History
  • 🗣️ ESL (English as a Second Language)
